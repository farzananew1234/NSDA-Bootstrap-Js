Our Packeges

Cox's Bazar

Cox’s Bazar is Bangladesh’s top tourist spot, famous for having the longest natural sea beach in the world. With golden sands, blue waves, beautiful sunsets, and nearby attractions like Inani and Himchari, it’s a perfect place for relaxation and adventure.

  • Duration: 3 Days/ 2 Nights
  • Price: BDT 12,000
  • Includes: Hotels, Breakfast, Sea Beach Visit

Sajek Vally

Sajek Valley is a stunning hill destination in Bangladesh, known as the “Queen of Hills.” It offers breathtaking views of clouds, green mountains, and peaceful nature. It’s perfect for those who love adventure, fresh air, and scenic beauty.

  • Duration: 3 Days/ 2 Nights
  • Price: BDT 10,000
  • Includes: Transport, Resort Stay, Sighrseeing

Sylhet

Sylhet is a beautiful region of Bangladesh known for its green tea gardens, rolling hills, rivers, and natural beauty. It’s peaceful, scenic, and rich in culture—perfect for nature lovers and travelers.

  • Duration: 3 Days/ 2 Nights
  • Price: BDT 11,000
  • Includes: Tea Garden, Jaflong, Rataegul
